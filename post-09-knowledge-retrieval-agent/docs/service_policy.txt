Meridian Advisory Group -- Client Service Policies

Version: 3.2
Effective Date: January 2026

Section 1: Engagement Terms

All client engagements with Meridian Advisory Group begin with a signed Statement of Work. No advisory work commences before the Statement of Work is countersigned by both parties. Verbal agreements do not constitute an active engagement under any circumstances.

Engagements are billed monthly in advance. Invoices are issued on the first business day of each month. Payment is due within 15 calendar days of the invoice date. Late payments beyond 30 days are subject to a 1.5 percent monthly service charge.

Section 2: Refund and Cancellation Policy

Clients may cancel an engagement with 30 calendar days written notice. Work completed prior to the cancellation date is billed at the agreed rate and is not eligible for refund. Prepaid monthly fees covering work not yet performed are refunded in full within 10 business days of confirmed cancellation.

Section 3: Confidentiality

All client information shared during an engagement is treated as strictly confidential. Meridian Advisory Group does not share, sell, or disclose client data to any third party without explicit written consent. All staff members sign individual non-disclosure agreements before client contact begins.

Section 4: Deliverable Acceptance

Deliverables are considered accepted if no written objection is received within 5 business days of delivery. Accepted deliverables are not eligible for rework under the original engagement terms. Change requests submitted after acceptance are scoped and billed as separate work items.

Section 5: Limitation of Liability

Meridian Advisory Group's total liability for any engagement shall not exceed the total fees paid by the client in the three months preceding the claim. Advisory outputs are professional recommendations and do not constitute legal, financial, or regulatory guarantees.
